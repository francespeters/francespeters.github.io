Project Overview

My AR navigation app prototype, NextStop AR, was designed in Figma to explore an innovative approach to navigating train stations after recognizing the unintuitive design of the Boston T. The interactive prototype leverages augmented reality to overlay real-time wayfinding cues, helping users seamlessly navigate complex station layouts. The design focuses on accessibility and clarity, ensuring a more intuitive transit experience
